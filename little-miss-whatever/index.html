LITTLE MISS WHATEVER

by Jackie Maldon

little miss whatever

by Jackie Maldon

One day a weird girl named Little Miss Whatever went on holidays to the Blue Mountains but she wanted to go to Canberra instead, and she said "Whatever!" because she didn't care.

When she got there everyone that saw her made fun of her, and she said "Whatever!".

At last no one laughed at her.

She happily went to her cabin.

When she saw everything she was very startled because everything was messy, but she didn't care. She just said "Whatever!".

When she had stayed there for one day and two nights Little Miss Whatever decided to go home.

When she got home she was so happy she never went anywhere again, and she said...

>

"Whatever!"

A little story about someone going to the Blue Mountains.
